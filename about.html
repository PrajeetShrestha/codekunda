About CodeKunda

We started in early 2022, and we're still growing. We're a small team of mobile developers who are totally passionate about mobile development and want to make it easy for everyone to learn and contribute to the mobile development community.

We work on a variety of projects, from small apps to large scale apps. We have expertise in native as well as cross-platform tools. We use technologies like React Native, Flutter, and Swift, Kotlin.

This site is a place where we share our knowledge and experience with each other.
