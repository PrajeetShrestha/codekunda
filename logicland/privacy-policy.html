LogicLand

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

CodeKunda ("we", "our", or "us") operates LogicLand, an educational iOS application designed for children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

LogicLand is designed for children and we take their privacy seriously. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All game data and progress is stored locally on the device
  • We do not require account creation or personal information to use the app
  • Parent/guardian consent is required for any optional features that may collect data

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Session history and metrics
  • App preferences and settings

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names or personal identifiers
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Photos or media
  • Contact information

In-App Purchases

LogicLand offers optional subscription plans. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of payment data.

Third-Party Services

LogicLand uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases
  • Apple AVFoundation: For text-to-speech functionality (processed locally on device)

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Using a device passcode
  • Enabling device encryption

Data Retention and Deletion

All app data can be deleted by:

  • Using the "Clear History" option in the Parent Dashboard
  • Uninstalling the application from your device

Parental Controls

LogicLand includes a Parent Gate to protect access to:

  • Subscription and purchase options
  • Settings and preferences
  • Progress data management

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
