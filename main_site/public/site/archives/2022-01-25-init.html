This marks the beginning of CodeKunda. For all these years I wanted to make a blog, share my experience, share my knowledge and archive it for myself and for the like mindeds. I wanted to get feedback from the community and validate my knowledge.

I explored lot of options, wordpress, tried to create a SPA with Angular or React. But it never came to fruition. Either due to lack of my knowledge or lack of flexibility of the selected blogging tool. I always found something missing in popular blogging tools.

Finally, I stumbled into Jekyll. And it clicked with me in an instant. It has a balance between flexibiliy that I wanted to have and ease of use. I am still learning it but so far loving it. The static nature of Jekyll is also great, since it means that the site loading is super fast and definitely give a obvious boost for SEO as well.

A lot of goodies on the pipeline stay tuned! Also if you have any suggestions and recommendations drop email at prajeet.shrestha@gmail.com
