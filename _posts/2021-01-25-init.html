This marks the beginning of CodeKunda. For all these years I wanted to make a blog, share my experience, share my knowledge and archive it for myself and for the like mindeds. I wanted to get feedback from the community and validate my knowledge.


I explored lot of options, wordpress, tried to create a SPA with Angular or React. But it never came to fruition. Either due to lack of my knowledge or lack of flexibility of the selected blogging tool.


Finally, I stumbled into Jekyll. And it clicked with me in an instant. It has a balance between flexibiliy that I wanted to have and ease of use. I am still learning it but so far loving it.
